[_Enter_ LADY CHILTERN _in walking dress_.]
LADY CHILTERN
Good afternoon, Lord Goring!
LORD GORING
Good afternoon, Lady Chiltern! Have you been in the Park?
LADY CHILTERN
No; I have just come from the Woman’s Liberal Association, where, by the way, Robert, your name was received with loud applause, and now I have come in to have my tea. [_To_ LORD GORING.]
You will wait and have some tea, won’t you?
